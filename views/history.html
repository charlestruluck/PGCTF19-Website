=== INIT HISTORY ===

I mean, no one will read this. Who cares?


Porter-Gaud Capture the Flag has a boring history. Like many start-ups, it was founded in a legend's garage- Tillson Galloway.

good_times :-)

The 2017 edition of PGCTF was the first. It was the start of an amazing journey for the team developing it, and the students who participated. Of course, the first of anything comes with mistakes- a fun fact about this year: the T-shirts handed out said, "PGCTF16" even though the event was actually held January 2017. We realized this mistake on the day of the event... That's fine though, we laugh at it looking back.

Unfortunately, we did not have a website this year.

A year after PGCTF17 we held "Copa de Porter". We were experienced and knew what to do- Things got serious. The day before the event rolls around and hardly anything had been set up yet. After spending countless hours at the school, we finish setup and head home. During the event, we got the brilliant idea of setting a computer in the middle of the room with a flag on it, and surprisingly it took a solid while before anyone even noticed it.
